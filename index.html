Passionate About Service

Established in 2011, Paul's Mini Workshop is a trusted leader in auto repairs, prioritizing exceptional customer service and building our reputation through word-of-mouth referrals. We hold accreditations from RMI and MIWA, and we partner with major mechanical warranty providers to ensure seamless service.

Our expertise shines in repairs for premium brands like Audi, BMW, Mini, and VW, though we welcome vehicles of all makes and models.

Our Services

We deliver comprehensive mechanical and electrical repairs, backed by our Celtic Tuning accreditation for advanced vehicle remaps, EGR, and DPF removal/deletion.

Vehicle Maintenance

Regular servicing is key to avoiding unexpected breakdowns—let us keep your vehicle running smoothly and efficiently.

Chip Tuning

Enhance your drive with professional remapping: boost performance, improve fuel economy, and unlock your engine's potential safely.
