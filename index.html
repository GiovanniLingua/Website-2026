Accounting & Bookkeeping Services

Business Proposal – Accounting & Bookkeeping

1. Introduction

This proposal outlines the accounting and bookkeeping services offered by DNA09 Trading (Pty) Ltd. Our objective is to provide accurate, compliant, and timely financial services that allow clients to focus on growing their business while we manage their financial records efficiently and professionally.

2. Scope of Services

2.1 Creditors Management
  • Posting and recording of creditor transactions
  • Reconciliation of creditor statements
  • Preparation and processing of creditor payments
  • Monitoring and management of outstanding creditor balances
2.2 Debtors Management
  • Generating and issuing debtor invoices
  • Preparation of debtor statements
  • Following up on outstanding debtor payments
  • Monitoring accounts receivable to improve cash flow
2.3 Bank Reconciliation
  • Monthly reconciliation of bank accounts
  • Identification and resolution of discrepancies
  • Ensuring accuracy between bank statements and accounting records
2.4 Financial Reporting
  • Preparation of the Trial Balance
  • Preparation of the Income Statement
  • Preparation of the Cash Flow Statement
  • Ensuring financial reports are accurate and ready for management review
2.5 Tax Compliance
  • Preparation and submission of VAT201 reports
  • Ensuring compliance with applicable tax regulations and deadlines

3. Value to the Client

  • Accurate and up-to-date financial records
  • Improved cash flow management
  • Reduced administrative burden
  • Compliance with statutory and tax requirements
  • Reliable financial information for decision-making

4. Service Delivery

All services are performed using professional accounting standards and best practices. We maintain strict confidentiality and ensure that all client information is handled securely and ethically.

5. Pricing

Pricing will be determined based on the volume of transactions, complexity of work, and specific client requirements. A detailed quotation will be provided upon assessment of the client's needs.

6. Conclusion

DNA09 Trading (Pty) Ltd is committed to delivering dependable and high-quality accounting and bookkeeping services. We look forward to supporting your business with accurate financial management and professional service delivery.
